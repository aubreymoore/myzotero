TROPICAL DEPRESSION CHABA (21W)
BULLETIN NO. 04
DATE: September 29, 2016
TIME: 8:30 AM

...

BASED ON INFORMATION RECEIVED FROM THE NATIONAL WEATHER SERVICE -TIYAN GUAM AND COMPILED AT THE CNMI EMERGENCY OPERATIONS CENTER-STATE WARNING POINT, TROPICAL STORM WATCH HAS BEEN CANCELLED FOR THE ISLANDS OF SAIPAN, TINIAN, ROTA AND GUAM.

...TROPICAL DEPRESSION CHABA STILL MOVING WEST AWAY FROM MARIANAS... LOCATION...14.5N 143.5E
ABOUT 155 MILES WEST-SOUTHWEST OF SAIPAN ABOUT 145 MILES WEST-SOUTHWEST OF TINIAN ABOUT 115 MILES WEST-NORTHWEST OF ROTA ABOUT 110 MILES NORTHWEST OF GUAM

MAXIMUM SUSTAINED WINDS...35 MPH

PRESENT MOVEMENT...WEST...280 DEGREES AT 13 MPH
AT 700 AM CHST...2100 UTC...THE CENTER OF TROPICAL DEPRESSION CHABA WAS LOCATED BY SATELLITE NEAR LATITUDE 14.5 DEGREES NORTH AND LONGITUDE 143.5 DEGREES EAST...MOVING WEST AT 13 MPH. CHABA WILL MOVE AWAY FROM THE MARIANAS ALONG A WESTWARD TRACK TODAY THEN TURN NORTHWEST WITH SLIGHTLY SLOWER FORWARD MOTION TONIGHT. MAXIMUM SUSTAINED WINDS REMAIN 35 MPH. CHABA IS EXPECTED TO SLOWLY STRENGTHEN AND BECOME A TROPICAL STORM THIS AFTERNOON.

ALTHOUGH TROPICAL DEPRESSION CHABA 21W IS MOVING WEST AND AWAY FROM THE MARIANAS AND A TROPICAL STORM WATCH HAS BEEN CANCELLED, GOVERNOR RALPH DLG. TORRES CONTINUES TO ADVISE RESIDENTS TO MAINTAIN NECESSARY PRECAUTIONARY MEASURES FOR GUSTY WINDS AND RAIN SHOWERS AS THEY ARE POSSIBLE.

THE CNMI EMERGENCY OPERATIONS CENTER STATE WARNING POINT WILL BE MONITORING THE MOVEMENT OF TROPICAL DEPRESSION 21W AND WILL BE ISSUING OUT BULLETINS AS THEY BECOME AVAILABLE. KEEP A CLOSE WATCH ON UPDATES TO WEATHER FORECASTS AND STAY INFORMED ON THE LATEST STATEMENTS AND ADVISORIES WHICH WILL BE AVAILABLE THROUGH LOCAL MEDIA SOURCES AND NOAA WEATHER RADIO BROADCAST ON 162.5 MEGAHERTZ, OR CALL CNMI EOC STATE WARNING POINT AT 237-8000 OR 664-8000, AND FOR THE NORTHERN ISLANDS TO CONTACT CNMI EOC STATE WARNING POINT AT HIGH FREQUENCY SINGLE SIDE BAND RADIO ON FREQUENCY 5.205.0.

THE NEXT SCHEDULED BULLETIN WILL BE ISSUED AT 2:00 PM LATER THIS AFTERNOON.EOC WARNING OFFICERS: JF/JS

Scheduled Power Interruption

The Commonwealth Utilities Corporation (CUC) would like to inform the general public that there will be a scheduled power interruption on Saturday, September 24, 2016, from 7:30 a.m. to 12:30 p.m.

The purpose of the power interruption is to de-energize the primary lines to allow the CUC line crew to safely relocate one (1) primary power pole and install two (2) primary power poles in Garapan along Beach Road. This is part of the system maintenan...ce work being performed throughout the island.

Areas to be affected: Feeder 2 (partial of 2nd partial):

Beach Road (Tony Romas South to Bank Pacific), Orchid St., Hibiscus St., Rose St., Bougainvilla Av., Dracaena St., Oleander St., Dama Di Noche St.,Mapola St., and Kadena Di Amor St.

Because of the scale of work that will be performed, we ask travelers to take alternate routes or to exercise caution when driving through the affected areas.

CUC apologizes for any inconvenience resulting from this power service interruption.

For more information, contact the CUC Customer Call Center (664-4282) or monitor our Facebook page for the latest updates (https://www.facebook.com/CommonwealthUtilitiesCorporation/).
