The University of Guam/College of Natural & Applied Sciences/Cooperative Extension Service will be hosting a workshop on "Home-making Yogurt" on Friday, December 4th, from 1pm-5pm at the UOG, Agriculture and Life Sciences building, room 125 (b & c). The workshop will cover technique and hands-on practice of making Plain and Greek yogurt as well as product tasting. Slots are limited and must register by calling 735-2029/2027 (or email: csnbarcinas@uguam.uog.edu) by December 3rd, 2015. A $20.00 registration fee covering materials and yogurt sampling will be collected on the workshop day.
